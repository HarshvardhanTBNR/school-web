'Best Faridabad Girl' in FIDE Rated Open Chess Tournament

Faridabad, Dec 27, 2019: Rijak Kaur from Apeejay School, Faridabad, was awarded 'Best Faridabad Girl' in FIDE Rated Open Chess Tournament, held from 23rd to 27th December 2019, at Eden Garden, Surajkund Road, Faridabad. The Tournament was organised by the All Faridabad Chess Association, on behalf of the All India Chess Federation (AICF).

Alumni

Faridabad, Sep. 2018: Apeejay School, Faridabad alumna Tanvi Agarwal, along with her husband, recently visited her alma mater. Tanvi was 'All India Topper (Rank - 1) of Class XII in the year 2009. She was the 'Head Girl' of the School and also the 'Vice President' of the Interact Club. She completed her Graduation from SRCC, followed by MBA from IIM, Bangalore. She is presently working with McKinsey and Company, Bangalore. The School Principal complimented her for outstanding achievements and for bringing laurels to the School.
